Steak

Description

How to pan-fry a steak

Seriously, though

Ingredients

  • steak
  • coarse salt
  • grit (as in determination, not silt)

Steps

  1. pre-heat a thick pan to medium-high
  2. Salt the steaks
  3. Throw the burner on max and throw the steaks on. Keep the meat moving for the first minute on high or '8'.
  4. Drop the heat to medium and let it sizzle for 1-4 minutes depending on thickness.
  5. Again increase the heat. Once the pan responds, flip that steak and move it around real good on high for 1 minute. Drop the heat and cook on medium for 1-4 min.
  6. let cool for 5min and serve
