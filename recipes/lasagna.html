Lasagna

Description

This dish is the product of ingenuity, laziness, extravagance, senility, and culture. Don't be fooled; you certainly are not eating spaghetti with extra cheese.

No, this architectural masterpiece is here to tell you: "I am somewhat reminiscent of a cake; do to me as you see fit."

Ingredients

  • lasagna pasta
  • ground beef
  • tomatus
  • cheez
  • grit (as in determination, not silt)

Steps

  1. slap the tomatoes
  2. chunk the tomatoes with the cheez
  3. using a large oven-safe casserole tray, layer the cheez, tomatus and pasta
  4. if you aren't out of grit yet, apply the remainder here
  5. serve in a smoothie glass with a thick straw
